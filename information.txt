\n The base url is: https://osband-duan-project1-database.herokuapp.com/
\n
\n Make sure you have a slash after every url for patch and post requests.
\n POST users/ creates a new user.
\n When you POST you must enter a "display_name", a "username", and the "latitude" (float) and
\n "longitude" (float) of the user's home base is optional and recommended.
\n GET users/ returns the list of all users with their display_name, username, and user_id.
\n PATCH users/<user_id>/ allows you to change the user's display_name, username,
\n and the latitude and longitude of their home base.
\n DELETE users/<user_id>/ deletes the user linked with user_id.
\n
\n POST locations/ creates a new location
\n When you POST you must enter an "id" (integer), a "place_title", the "address", the "city",
\n the "state", and the "zip_code" (integer).
\n Latitude and longitude of the place of interest will be automatically generated.
\n GET locations/ returns the list of each user and id corresponding to a dictionary of
\n all locations associated with that user_id. For each location it lists the place_title, latitude, and longitude.
\n The home base is automatically added as one of the user's pois.
\n GET <user_id>/poi/ returns the list of poi's associated with that user_id with their
\n place_title, latitude and longitude.
\n PATCH locations/<user_id>/<placeTitle>/ allows you to change the locations's place_title, address, city,
\n state, and zip_code.
\n DELETE locations/<user_id>/<placeTitle>/ deletes the locations linked with user_id.
